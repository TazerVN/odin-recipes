Milktea

Description

Yummers! I love my milk tea ummm yum yum here is the recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons loose black tea or 2 tea bags (like Assam or Earl Grey)
  • 1 cup milk (whole, almond, or your preferred type)
  • Sweetener (sugar, honey, or condensed milk) to taste
  • Optional: spices like cardamom or cinnamon for added flavor

Instruction:

  1. Boil the Water:

    • In a saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.

  2. Brew the Tea:

    • Once boiling, remove the saucepan from heat and add the tea leaves or tea bags.
    • Let it steep for about 5-7 minutes, depending on how strong you want the tea.

  3. Add Milk:

    • After steeping, strain the tea to remove the leaves or remove the tea bags.
    • Return the tea to the saucepan and add the milk. Stir to combine.

  4. Sweeten:

    • Add your desired sweetener to taste, stirring until fully dissolved. If using spices, add them at this stage.

  5. Heat (Optional):

    • If you prefer your milk tea hot, gently heat it on the stove until warm, but do not let it boil.

  6. Serve:

    • Pour the milk tea into cups and enjoy! You can also serve it over ice for a refreshing iced milk tea.

Variations:

  • Iced Milktea: Allow the tea to cool and serve over ice.
  • Bubble Tea: Add cooked tapioca pearls for a fun twist.
  • Flavored Milk Tea: Experiment with different teas or add flavored syrups.
