Beef Pho

Description

Beef pho is a delicious Vietnam beef noodle soup and here is how you can make it at home

Ingredients:

For the Broth:

  • 2 pounds of beef bones (preferably knuckle or marrow bones)
  • 1 pound brisket or flank steak
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 1 piece of ginger (about 3 inches), halved lengthwise
  • 5-6 whole star anise
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Fish sauce (to taste)
  • Water (about 4-5 quarts)

For the Soup:

  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • Fresh herbs (basil, cilantro, mint)
  • Lime wedges
  • Bean sprouts
  • Sliced jalape√±os or other chillies
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Hoisin sauce and sriracha (optional)

Instruction:

  1. Prepare the Broth:

    1. Char the Aromatics: Place the halved onion and ginger on a baking sheet and broil until charred (about 10 minutes). This adds depth to the broth.
    2. Blanch the bones:In a dry skillet or over an open flame, char the onion and ginger until slightly blackened. This adds depth to the broth.
    3. Simmer the brothIn a large pot, add the cleaned bones, charred onion and ginger, star anise, cloves, cinnamon stick, coriander seeds, salt, and enough water to cover everything. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cook for at least 4-6 hours (the longer, the better!). You can also use a slow cooker for about 8-10 hours.
    4. Strain the broth:Once the broth is rich and flavorful, strain it through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth into another pot, discarding the solids.
    5. Season the broth:Add fish sauce and adjust the seasoning to your taste. Keep it warm.

  2. Prepare the Noodles:

    • Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  3. Assemble the dish:

    1. In a bowl, add a portion of the cooked rice noodles.
    2. Top with thinly sliced raw beef (it will cook when you pour the hot broth over it).
    3. Ladle hot broth over the noodles and beef.
    4. Garnish with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime wedges, sliced chili peppers, and green onions.
    5. Serve with hoisin sauce and sriracha on the side

Tips:

  • For extra flavor, consider adding a splash of soy sauce or additional fish sauce.
  • Customize your toppings based on your preferences!

Enjoy your Pho!
